Data Analytics Lead, Global Services Responsibilities

    Design, develop, and maintain data models, queries, reports, and visualizations to support data-driven decision-making and optimization of our global workplaces.
    Lead data and analytics efforts within Global Workplace Services and cross-functional teams, collaborating with stakeholders to understand data needs and representing key insights in a meaningful way.
    Optimize pipelines, dashboards, frameworks, and systems to facilitate easier development of data artifacts and streamline our data analytics processes.
    Deploy better Analytics engineering principles to maintain team and partner alignment.
    Mentor team members by providing actionable feedback and guidance, fostering continuous learning and improvement.

Minimum Qualifications

    Bachelor's degree in a directly related field, or equivalent practical experience.
    8+ years of work experience involving analytical rigor as typically seen in functions like finance, data science, strategy, business operations, or related fields (or 6+ years of analysis work experience with Masters degree)
    Advanced SQL skills with experience working with large datasets
    Experience with data querying languages (e.g., SQL), data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau), ETL, and data modeling.
    Demonstrated analytical skills, including both qualitative and quantitative analysis techniques (e.g., regression analysis, cluster analysis, hypothesis testing)
    Experience initiating and completing analytical projects with minimal guidance, and present technical content to non-technical audiences and leadership

Preferred Qualifications

    Graduate degree in Mathematics, Statistics, a relevant technical field, or equivalent practical experience in data analytics field.
    Proven experience in data analysis, reporting, and visualization, including merging disparate data sets, identifying correlations, and presenting findings in various formats.
    Proficient in at least one programming language (e.g., Python, R).
    Experience working in fast-paced, dynamic environments with a focus on operations and facilities management, preferably in a global organization with multiple locations.
